Quiz Memo

Review the correct answers for each question in the engineering quiz.

Memo

1. What is the primary focus of mechanical engineering?

Answer: The design and development of heavy machinery.

2. Which type of engineering involves the use of electricity to power digital systems?

Answer: Electrical Engineering

3. What does civil engineering primarily focus on?

Answer: The creation and maintenance of infrastructure.

4. Which field of engineering is concerned with the creation of new medical devices?

Answer: Biomedical Engineering

5. What is the main focus of software engineering?

Answer: The creation of new software applications.

6. Which field deals with designing and maintaining computer hardware?

Answer: Computer Engineering

7. Which type of engineering focuses on renewable energy sources?

Answer: Environmental Engineering

8. What does chemical engineering mainly focus on?

Answer: The development and manufacturing of chemicals and materials.

9. Which engineering field focuses on the study of the environment and natural resources?

Answer: Environmental Engineering

10. What is the primary goal of aerospace engineering?

Answer: Designing and developing aircraft and spacecraft.
